# Kintore Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-04-20_

Kintore 是一款完全在本機運作的訓練紀錄 app。本政策說明我們收集什麼、存在哪裡、如何保護，以及您對自己資料的權利。

Kintore is a workout-logging app that operates entirely on your device. This policy explains what we collect, where it is stored, how it is protected, and your rights over your data.

---

## 1. 摘要 / Summary

- **我們不收集任何資料到伺服器。** Kintore 沒有帳號系統，沒有後端，所有資料只存在您的 iPhone 本機。
- **We do not collect any data on any server.** Kintore has no account system and no backend. All data lives on your device.
- **您可以隨時匯出（JSON / CSV）或永久刪除您的資料。**
- **You can export your data (JSON / CSV) or delete it permanently at any time.**

---

## 2. 我們儲存什麼 / What We Store

所有資料以 SQLite 資料庫檔 `gymflow.sqlite` 的形式存在 Kintore 自己的 app sandbox 裡。包括：

All data is stored as a SQLite database `gymflow.sqlite` inside Kintore's app sandbox. This includes:

- 您建立的訓練紀錄（日期、動作、組數、重量、次數、時長、距離）  
  Workouts you create (date, exercises, sets, weight, reps, duration, distance)
- 您自訂的訓練動作與課表  
  Custom exercises and routines you create
- 個人最佳紀錄（PR）  
  Personal records (PRs)
- App 設定（單位、語言、休息秒數、外觀偏好）  
  App settings (units, language, rest timer, appearance)
- 本機操作日誌（分析用，用於偵測崩潰與效能問題，只存本機）  
  A local event log for analytics (used to detect crashes and performance issues, stored only on your device)

我們**不會**蒐集姓名、email、電話、地理位置、聯絡人、相片或其他個人身分資料。

We do **not** collect your name, email, phone number, location, contacts, photos, or any other personally identifiable information.

---

## 3. Apple Health（選填）/ Apple Health (Optional)

若您在 Kintore 的**設定 → Apple 健康**啟用並授權，Kintore 會在您結束一次訓練時，把**訓練時長**與**活動類型**寫入 Apple Health。

If you enable and authorize **Settings → Apple Health** in Kintore, the app will write your **workout duration** and **activity type** to Apple Health when you finish a session.

- Kintore **不會讀取** Apple Health 裡的任何資料  
  Kintore does **not read** any data from Apple Health
- 您可以在 iOS **設定 → 隱私權與安全性 → 健康 → Kintore** 隨時撤銷此權限  
  You can revoke this permission at any time in iOS **Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → Kintore**
- 預設為關閉。  
  This is off by default.

---

## 4. 資料不會離開您的裝置 / Data Never Leaves Your Device

Kintore **不**使用任何雲端服務、分析平台、廣告網絡、或第三方 SDK 來傳輸您的資料。我們沒有伺服器、沒有跨裝置同步、沒有網路請求（除非未來版本明確告知）。

Kintore does **not** use any cloud services, analytics platforms, ad networks, or third-party SDKs that transmit your data. There is no server, no cross-device sync, and no network requests (unless a future version explicitly discloses it).

---

## 5. 您的控制權 / Your Controls

- **匯出**：設定 → 備份 → 匯出 JSON 或 CSV，透過 iOS 分享表單存到 Files、Mail、AirDrop 等任何地方  
  **Export**: Settings → Backup → Export JSON or CSV, then share via Files, Mail, AirDrop, etc.
- **刪除個別紀錄**：任何一組或一整次訓練都可從 App 裡直接刪除  
  **Delete individual records**: any set or entire workout can be deleted from within the app
- **刪除全部資料**：從 iPhone 刪除 Kintore app 會一併清除所有本機資料  
  **Delete everything**: uninstalling Kintore from your iPhone removes all local data

---

## 6. 兒童 / Children

Kintore 沒有針對 13 歲以下兒童設計，也不會刻意蒐集他們的資料。

Kintore is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

We will update the date at the top of this page when material changes are made. Please check back periodically.
